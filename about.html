Why We Created the Site

We want to share what we know now

This site helps other people to learn what we learned and what made us better students.

A Helpful Aspect

Not just for us being a student, bus also in life. This site enabled us to practice and test our skills and knowledge regarding the subject. Not only did we get to see what we can do, but it also improved along the way, making us better students in the field.

Also a helpful aspect in our lives. This site will be an achievement and will be a product of what we have worked hard for. This site enabled the students to be more collaborative, hard-working, intuitive, and many more that will be an impact in the future.

We Are Passionate About Web

The Web is an amazing place where you can find anything you want, we want to contribute to a place where people can both enjoy and learn. We want people to explore the Web and be informed in the process.

About the Site

This website is created by Information Technology students of Saint Louis University.
This site aims to help users to gain knowledge about web developing, server-client interaction of computers using web services, and to understand the different responses that the server sent
